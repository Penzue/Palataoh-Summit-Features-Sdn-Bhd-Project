About Us

Summit Features Sdn. Bhd. (SFSB) is a local company specialising in motion tracking technology for scientific applications and the creative industry. SFSB is Southeast Asia's number one motionn capture integrator and has been operating since 2005. We offer a unique and easily accessible motion capture systems for human movement research, visual effects, augmented reality development and object tracking. Our mission is to develop and empower local expertise together to enhance Malaysia achievement globally in areas such as sports, medical, industry, education and etc.
