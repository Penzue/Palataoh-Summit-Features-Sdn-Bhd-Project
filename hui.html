Vision, Mission, Strategy

VISION

To be a sustainable leader in scientific fields related to human movement and performance studies to contribute to nation’s growth and building towards year 2030.

MISSION

Develop and empower local expertise together with local higher education institutions as catalyst to enhance Malaysia achievement globally in areas such as sports, medical, industry, education and etc.

STRATEGY

  • Develop partnership with foreign expertise for transfer technologies and developing local expertise.
  • Collaborations with local higher education institutions in research studies, testing and training.
  • Setting up technology center and laboratory, comprise of state-of-the-art equipment’s and experts.

Our Contributions

OUR CONTRIBUTIONS

SPORTS FACULTY, UPSI

A Biomechanics Lab was established at the Faculty of Sports Science and Coaching, consisting of six Vicon T-series optical cameras with one Vicon Bonita reference video camera. This Vicon motion capture system is integrated with two AMTI Optima force plates to give data on ground reaction forces.

RCMP UNIKL

Royal College of Medicine Perak (RCMP) had purchased Vicon motion capture system with eight Vero optical camera and one Vue reference camera with Nexus 2 software to teach students on the kinematic data obtained using the system for applications such as gait analysis and sports performance.

ASWARA WORKSHOP

SFSB organized two motion capture workshops in 2018 and 2019 which was conducted by a team of experts from Vicon UK and Mr. Sina Azad, Technical Director at Plastic Wax. Participants familiarized themselves with capturing mocap data which was then used to create a short animated story from the mocap data.
